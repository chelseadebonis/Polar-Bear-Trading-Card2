LE BEAR POLAR

Polar bears clean themselves by rolling in the snow!

  • Scientific Name: Ursus Maritimus
  • Average Weight: 550-900 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: Artic Zones

There are an estimate of 20,000-25,000 polar bears in the world. They live in the Arctic and tend to find habitats close to ice breaks to feed easily on seals. Unlike other bear breeds, polar bears do not hibernate in the winter. POLAR BEARS ARE BRILLIANT!
